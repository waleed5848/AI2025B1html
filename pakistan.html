Welcome to Pakistan

Pakistan is a culturally rich and geographically diverse country located in South Asia. It consists of five provinces: Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Balochistan, and Gilgit-Baltistan. Each province offers unique landscapes, culture, and heritage.

Provinces of Pakistan

  • Punjab
  • Sindh
  • Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK)
  • Balochistan
  • Gilgit-Baltistan
